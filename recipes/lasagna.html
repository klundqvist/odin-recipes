Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular italian dish sort of like a cake but with pasta encapsulating a wonderful bolognese sauce

Ingredients

  • Lasagne plates
  • Bolognese
  • Bechamel
  • Parmiggiano

Steps

  1. Put the oven to 225 degrees celsius
  2. Layer lasagna plates, bolognese and bechamel 3 times
  3. Finish with the parmiggiano
  4. Broil in oven for 45 minutes
